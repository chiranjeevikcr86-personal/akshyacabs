Two decades of devotion, trust, and seamless journeys — rooted in Tirupati, reaching every sacred corner of India.

Our Founder

S. Ravi Kumar

Founder & Managing Director, Akshaya Cabs

With over 20 years of dedicated experience in the travel industry, S. Ravi Kumar built Akshaya Cabs from the ground up with a single vision — to make every pilgrimage and leisure journey from Tirupati a truly blessed and seamless experience.

Born and raised in Tirupati, Ravi Kumar garu has an intimate knowledge of every pilgrim route, sacred site, and hidden gem across South India and beyond. His passion for service, combined with deep spiritual values, has earned Akshaya Cabs the trust of thousands of families over the years.

Under his leadership, the company has expanded from local Tirupati tours to pan-India packages including Char Dham, Aru Padai Yatra, and Pancha Bhoota Lingas — always maintaining the same personal touch and commitment to quality.
